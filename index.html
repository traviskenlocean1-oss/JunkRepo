Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does pricing work?
    We price by volume — how much space your junk takes up in our truck. We give you a firm quote before starting, so there are never any surprises. No hidden fees.
  • Do you offer same-day service?
    Yes! Same-day service is available most days across Orlando and surrounding areas. Call us early in the day to secure your slot.
  • What items do you NOT take?
    We cannot haul hazardous materials such as chemicals, paint, asbestos, or biohazardous waste. Everything else is fair game — furniture, appliances, electronics, debris, yard waste, and more.
  • Do I need to be home during pickup?
    Not necessarily. As long as we have access to the junk and you've approved the quote, our crew can handle the job without you being present.
  • What happens to my junk after pickup?
    We sort everything at our facility. Usable items go to local donation centers. Recyclable materials are diverted from landfills. Only what can't be donated or recycled goes to disposal.

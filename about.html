Our Story

Started with a Truck
and a Promise

SwiftHaul started in 2018 when our founder Marcus saw how difficult it was for Orlando homeowners and landlords to find reliable junk removal that actually showed up on time. He started with one truck, one crew member, and a simple promise: fair prices, same-day service, and no leaving a mess behind.

Since then, SwiftHaul has grown into one of Central Florida's most trusted junk removal companies. We've handled everything from single-item pickups to full estate cleanouts, commercial property clear-outs, and post-renovation debris removal.

Today our crew of 12 serves Orlando and 12 surrounding cities, completing 2,500+ removals per year with a 4.9-star Google rating built review by review.

We're not a franchise. We're your neighbors — and we take pride in every single job.

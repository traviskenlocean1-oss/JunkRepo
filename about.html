Our Story

Started with a Truck
and a Promise

SwiftHaul started in 2018 when our founder Marcus saw how difficult it was for Orlando homeowners and landlords to find reliable junk removal that actually showed up on time. He started with one truck, one crew member, and a simple promise: fair prices, same-day service, and no leaving a mess behind.

Since then, SwiftHaul has grown into one of Central Florida's most trusted junk removal companies. We've handled everything from single-item pickups to full estate cleanouts, commercial property clear-outs, and post-renovation debris removal.

Today our crew of 12 serves Orlando and 12 surrounding cities, completing 2,500+ removals per year with a 4.9-star Google rating built review by review.

We're not a franchise. We're your neighbors — and we take pride in every single job.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

HOW DOES YOUR PRICING WORK?
We price by volume — how much space your junk takes up in our truck. We give you a firm quote before we ever start, so there are zero surprises. No hidden fees, no add-ons at the end of the job.
DO YOU OFFER SAME-DAY SERVICE?
Yes — same-day service is available most days across Orlando and surrounding areas. Call us early in the day and we'll do our best to fit you in the schedule. Same-day is our default, not an upgrade.
WHAT ITEMS DO YOU NOT REMOVE?
We cannot haul hazardous materials such as chemicals, paint, asbestos, motor oil, or biohazardous waste. Everything else is fair game — furniture, appliances, electronics, debris, yard waste, mattresses, and more.
DO I NEED TO BE HOME DURING PICKUP?
Not necessarily. As long as we have access to the junk and you've approved the quote, our crew can handle the job without you being on-site. Many customers leave the gate unlocked and we handle the rest.
ARE YOU LICENSED AND INSURED?
Yes. SwiftHaul is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of Florida. Your home and property are protected throughout every job we do — from the moment our truck arrives to the moment we leave.
WHAT HAPPENS TO MY JUNK AFTER PICKUP?
We sort everything at our facility. Usable items go to local donation centers and charities. Recyclable materials are diverted from the landfill. Only what truly can't be saved or recycled goes to disposal.
HOW BIG IS YOUR TRUCK?
Our trucks hold the equivalent of a standard 16-foot moving truck. We offer quarter, half, three-quarter, and full loads. Most residential jobs — a garage or estate cleanout — fill a half to full load.
DO YOU HAUL FROM UPSTAIRS OR BASEMENTS?
Absolutely. Our crew handles all the heavy lifting — stairways, attics, basements, or tight spaces. Just show us what needs to go and we take it from there. No extra charge for stairs in most cases.
CAN YOU REMOVE JUST ONE ITEM?
Yes. We handle single-item pickups all the time — a couch, mattress, refrigerator, or hot tub. Our minimum charge covers a small portion of the truck. No job is too small for SwiftHaul.
DO YOU OFFER SENIOR OR MILITARY DISCOUNTS?
Yes — we offer discounts for senior citizens and active or veteran military members. Just mention it when you call and we'll apply it to your quote. It's our way of giving back to the community.
HOW FAR IN ADVANCE DO I NEED TO BOOK?
We accept same-day bookings most days. For larger jobs like full estate or commercial cleanouts, booking 24–48 hours ahead ensures we can allocate the right crew size. Call early for best availability.
DO YOU WORK ON WEEKENDS?
Yes. We operate Monday through Saturday and Sundays by appointment. Weekend slots fill fast — especially Saturdays — so call ahead if you need weekend service.
WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?
We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, Venmo, Zelle, and Apple Pay. Payment is due at the completion of the job, after you've approved the work. We never collect payment upfront.
DO YOU HANDLE CONSTRUCTION DEBRIS?
Yes. We specialize in post-construction and renovation cleanup — drywall, lumber, tile, concrete, roofing materials, and more. We work with contractors and homeowners across Central Florida on a regular basis.
CAN YOU CLEAR OUT A STORAGE UNIT?
Absolutely. Storage unit cleanouts are one of our most requested services. We haul out everything, sort what can be donated or recycled, and leave the unit completely empty and clean. Same-day availability in most cases.
DO YOU REMOVE HOT TUBS OR LARGE ITEMS?
Yes. We remove hot tubs, above-ground pools, swing sets, playground equipment, and other large outdoor items. These jobs may require extra crew members — just let us know what you have when you call.
IS THERE A MINIMUM CHARGE?
Yes, we have a small minimum that covers a single item or a very small load. The exact amount varies by location and item type. Call us and we'll give you an honest estimate right over the phone in most cases.
HOW LONG DOES A TYPICAL JOB TAKE?
Most standard residential jobs take 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on volume. Full estate or property cleanouts can take a half day or more. We always give you a time estimate when we arrive and review the job.
DO YOU SERVE AREAS OUTSIDE OF ORLANDO?
Yes — we serve all of Central Florida including Kissimmee, Winter Park, Altamonte Springs, Sanford, Ocoee, Apopka, Lake Mary, Windermere, Celebration, Clermont, and Longwood. Call to confirm availability in your area.
WHAT SHOULD I DO TO PREPARE FOR PICKUP?
Not much. Just make sure we can access the items — clear a path if needed and let us know if there are parking restrictions. You don't need to move anything yourself. That's exactly what we're here for.
